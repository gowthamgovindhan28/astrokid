AstroKid: The Return Journey

Developed by Gowtham G

About the Game

Welcome to AstroKid: The Return Journey! In this game, you’ll guide a young astronaut boy as he navigates a whimsical landscape filled with unique challenges. Instead of facing dangerous obstacles, AstroKid encounters quirky phenomena, like making things wet or dealing with sizeable holes in the ground.

Explore this vibrant terrain and use your skill and quick reflexes to cleverly maneuver through these playful challenges, all in a quest to reunite him with his home.

Controls

  • Move Right: Press D
  • Move Left: Press A
  • Jump: Press W
  • Kid's Voice: Press S

Master these controls to help AstroKid navigate safely and avoid the unknown perils of this foreign landscape.

Game Levels

This is just the beginning! You’re currently playing Level 1, where you’ll get accustomed to the landscape and the basic controls. Future levels will present more difficult terrains, introducing new obstacles and surprises.

About the Developer

This game was created by Gowtham G, a passionate web developer skilled in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. AstroKid: The Return Journey is a project inspired by the adventure of exploring the unknown while finding a way home.
